About sdx1.net

Updated 2020-09-15. Written by sdx1 on 2017-08-06.

sdx1.net is a simple website for archiving useful and interesting things. In an age of blogs, forums, wikis, subreddits, imageboards, and social networks controlled by massive data-mining conglomerates, it's nice to control a tiny, simple, untracked, compatible, anachronistic piece of the internet, armed with a text editor and an FTP client. Especially on a four-letter dot net domain.

The Name

It's a reference to the naming scheme for hard drives on modern Linux systems - disk X (#25), partition 1 would be /dev/sdx1.

Design Goals

  • Readability on any platform - even IE6, or your favorite text editor
  • Elegance - beautiful websites can be small
  • Security - no scripting when avoidable, either in PHP or JS
  • Independence - everything loads from my server (or your hard drive)
